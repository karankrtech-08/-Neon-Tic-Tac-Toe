ğŸ® How to Play
Basic Rules
Players take turns placing X and O on a 3x3 grid
First player to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins
If all 9 squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw

Game Modes
Player vs Player: Challenge a friend on the same device
Player vs Computer: Play against AI with adjustable difficulty

Difficulty Levels
Easy: AI makes occasional mistakes
Medium: Balanced AI with strategic play
Hard: Advanced AI using minimax algorithm (unbeatable)
